Why Honeyhive is the canonical 1st-sibling

Honeyhive is the ONLY ai_eval_observability vendor that ships chain-poll multi-judge consensus as the primary eval layer. Where Galileo Luna-2 (lead 286) scores a trace with a single LLM-as-judge and Arize Phoenix (lead 101) scores with heuristic + LLM-mix and Fiddler Trust Service (lead 292) scores with custom-metric + LLM-judge hybrid, Honeyhive explicitly fires N judge models in sequence and returns a consensus decision — which means the audit-trail surface you have to reconstruct is fundamentally wider. Auditors will not just ask "what did the judge say" — they will ask "which judges fired, in what order, with what temperature and prompt-template-version, and what consensus threshold produced the final score." That is an 11-column join-table, not a 5-column one.

The Canonical 11-Column Honeyhive Join-Table

  1. per_trace_id — logical trace (Honeyhive tracing anchor)
  2. per_span_id — distributed-trace edge inside the trace
  3. per_judge_model_id + per_judge_model_version_id — which judge model fired (Hallucination, Context Relevance, Faithfulness, Answer Relevance, Toxicity, Bias, custom)
  4. per_judge_model_temperature — temperature pin at audit time (the most-asked column by SOC 2 auditors)
  5. per_judge_model_prompt_template_version_id — immutable hash of the prompt-template + judge-instructions
  6. per_judge_model_output + per_judge_model_confidence — raw judge response + judge self-reported confidence
  7. per_consensus_decision — chain-poll consensus vote (majority / unanimous / weighted / threshold)
  8. per_consensus_confidence — derived confidence in the consensus decision itself
  9. per_ground_truth_id — golden-set row used as the ground-truth anchor
  10. per_human_review_id — human reviewer override + rationale (the EU AI Act Art. 14 human-oversight column)
  11. per_eval_run_id — logical eval run grouping all judges + consensus + human-review

  2. Judge-model + golden-set + fine-tune-corpus license-provenance. Required by EU AI Act Art. 53(d) GPAI training-data-summary, Art. 53(1)(b) copyright-summary, ISO 42001 §6.1.4. Auditors will probe each judge's training-data license + the golden-set license + the per-customer-fine-tune-corpus license.
  3. Prompt-injection + judge-model-bypass + chain-poll-consensus-bypass + golden-set-poisoning + per-eval-run-replay defense. Required by OWASP LLM Top 10 LLM01 + LLM03 + LLM04 + LLM06, MITRE ATLAS, NIST AI RMF MEASURE, EU AI Act Art. 9 + Art. 14. The Honeyhive-specific attack-surface: a malicious actor who poisons a subset of judge-models in the chain-poll can flip the consensus without flipping any individual judge. Auditors will probe this with a 10-column per-trace-id join-table.
  4. Cross-tenant Honeyhive SaaS isolation evidence. Required by SOC 2 CC6.1, GDPR Art. 28, HIPAA, EU AI Act Art. 10, FedRAMP Moderate. Honeyhive Logs ingests prompt + completion pairs — meaning the audit-trail surface includes tenant-isolation-of-prompt-completion-pairs, which is a higher bar than just tenant-isolation-of-evals.
  5. WORM retention + per-data-asset-PII-classification + EU AI Act Annex III §4 high-risk-classification + GDPR Art. 17 deletion-propagation. The chain-poll judge outputs are decisions about AI-system behavior. If a Honeyhive-using customer's AI is making credit / employment / healthcare / law-enforcement / essential-services decisions (Annex III §4), then the Honeyhive eval trail is itself a high-risk-classification evidence artifact and Art. 17 deletion-propagation has to flow through the chain-poll store.
