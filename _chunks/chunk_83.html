  7. Document Apache Kafka + Apache Kafka Streams + Apache ksqlDB training-corpus + fine-tune license-provenance per EU AI Act Art. 53(d) + Art. 53(1)(b) + Art. 53(2).

  11. Produce the WORM billing + cost-attribution join-table per Audit gap #5.
  12. Map downstream-Snowflake/Databricks/BigQuery/Redshift/Postgres/Iceberg/Hudi/Delta/S3-state-change to per-Kafka-Connect-CDC-sink state-change events.
  13. Map downstream-Kafka-topic-state-change to per-Confluent-AI-function-call + per-Confluent-Warpstream-AI-function-call events.
