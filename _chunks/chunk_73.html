1. Why this checklist exists (the audit-evidence problem no single vendor in the ai_sales_ai pipeline solves end-to-end)

Every production end-to-end-AI-revenue-platform deployed at a regulated enterprise today inherits at least 5 audit-evidence surfaces simultaneously: (a) SOC 2 CC7.2 — the operational-component "system operations" criterion that requires monitoring of system components + detection of failures + response to incidents; (b) EU AI Act Art. 12 — record-keeping obligations for high-risk AI systems requiring automatic logging of events over the system's lifecycle; (c) EU AI Act Art. 14 — human-oversight requirements designed to prevent or minimise the risks to health, safety or fundamental rights; (d) EU AI Act Art. 50 — transparency obligations for AI systems intended to interact directly with natural persons; (e) EU AI Act Art. 53(d) — GPAI training-data-summary obligations for general-purpose AI models.
