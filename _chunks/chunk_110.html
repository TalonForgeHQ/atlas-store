  1. Locate the privacy-policy surface. Canonical anchor: mercury.com/legal/privacy.   2. Initiate vendor-DD outreach via the help@ channel. Mercury's help@ distribution is monitored by both the legal team (privacy + DPA + EU AI Act readiness) and the security team (SOC 2 attestation + ISO 27001 status + pen-test summary). The vendor-DD approach via help@ produces faster, more substantive responses than cold-outreach to AEs.
  3. Ask for the banking-compliance + EU AI Act readiness packet. The packet includes: BSA / AML / OFAC program documentation, SOC 2 Type II report, ISO 27001 certificate, GDPR DPA, EU AI Act Art. 53(d) GPAI training-data-transparency for Mercury AI, Annex III risk-classification, ISO 42001 audit-status; expected turnaround 5-10 business days.
  4. Validate SOC 2 Type II scope — confirm the audit window covers the 12 months preceding your contract start, and that the audit scope includes all of Mercury Business Checking + Mercury Treasury + Mercury AP + Mercury IO + Mercury Pay + Mercury Cards + Mercury AI (not just the management plane).
  5. Validate ISO 27001 status — confirm whether the certificate is already awarded (Mercury is certified per mercury.com/security); capture the certificate number and the cert body.
  6. Validate GDPR DPA + Annex III + Art. 22 + Art. 53(d) — request the signed DPA, the per-call PII-handling notice for Mercury AI cash-flow-forecasting, the per-card-transaction fraud-detection-ML model-card, and the Mercury AI training-corpus-source + per-customer-financial-data license-provenance summary.
  7. Validate data-residency posture — confirm US vs EU vs multi-region cluster availability, per-customer data-segregation, per-customer multi-entity-state, per-Mercury-IO multi-entity-transfer-isolation, and the partner-bank Choice Financial Group + Evolve Bank & Trust data-segregation evidence.
  8. Validate billing + cost-attribution transparency — Mercury exposes per-card-transaction + per-international-wire + per-bill-pay-ACH-event + per-Mercury-Treasury-money-market-sweep-event feeds for cost-attribution. Confirm you can export these feeds into your downstream-Snowflake / BigQuery / Databricks / Sage Intacct / NetSuite / Xero / QBO cost-allocation pipeline, including WORM retention per SEC 17a-4 + IRS 6001 + SOX-analog-considerations.
