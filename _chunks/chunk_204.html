28-column audit checklist

  1. per-model-weights-hash-id — confirm SHA-256 + BLAKE3 hashes are captured for every model artifact, not just the top-level hash from the registry.
  2. per-model-version-id — confirm version lineage is captured (model-v1.0 · model-v1.1 · model-v2.0-beta) for rollback + audit reconstruction.
  3. per-model-registry-source-id — required to distinguish Hugging Face · PyTorch Hub · TensorFlow Hub · ONNX Model Zoo · internal-registry sources.

  5. per-artifact-scan-result-id — confirm scan-result linkage (clean · backdoor-detected · trojan-detected · weight-poisoning-detected · suspicious-pattern).
  6. per-prompt-injection-detection-signal-id — HiddenLayer AIShield captures this at the LLM-input layer; confirm per-event capture not aggregated.

  10. per-policy-rule-id — required to identify which detection-rule fired (backdoor-pattern · trojan-pattern · weight-poisoning-pattern).
