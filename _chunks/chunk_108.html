Drift (Salesloft) Conversational AI + EU AI Act Art. 14 + Art. 22 + SOC 2 Vendor-DD Audit (2026)

If you sell a conversational-AI buyer-bot or a Drift-style routing bot into Fortune 500 B2B-SaaS accounts in 2026, you are in scope for EU AI Act Article 14 (human oversight) plus Article 22 (prohibited automated decisions) plus Annex III Category 4 (emotion recognition in buyer intent) if your bot infers buyer engagement-affect from the conversation. Drift — now part of Salesloft post the Feb 2024 $2.7B merger — is the canonical Tier-1 platform here with 7,000+ paying customers, Drift AI Site Concierge + Drift Prospector + Drift Fastlane + Drift Audiences + Drift Video + Drift Intel + Drift Email in a single stack, and a Salesloft-Drift-cross-product tenant-mapping that makes the SOC 2 + GDPR + EU AI Act evidence chain unusually complex.

The Art. 14 + Art. 22 evidence chain for Drift

Article 14 requires documented human oversight at every point where an AI system materially influences a downstream decision. Article 22 prohibits automated decisions that materially affect a buyer based solely on AI output without meaningful human review. For a Drift-style conversational-AI buyer-bot platform, that means at minimum six logged checkpoints per conversation:

  1. Buyer-message → Drift AI reply (bot-suggested vs bot-auto-sent vs rep-edited toggle + logged model-version + cited-knowledge-base-doc-id + confidence-threshold + per-rep-approval-toggle per EU AI Act Art. 12 + Art. 14)
  2. Drift Prospector lead-routing → Salesloft SDR (logged ML-rank-score + rep-availability + timezone-overlay + account-tier + ICP-match + Drift-Audiences-account-fit + GDPR-consent-bit + EU-caller-consent-bit per GDPR Art. 22 + Art. 28 + CCPA/CPRA)
  3. Drift Fastlane / Concierge meeting-booking-decision (slot-eligibility-set + conflict-detection + buffer-rule + working-hours-rule + OOO-override + Drift-rep-vs-Salesloft-SDR-routing-decision + Drift-Audiences-account-fit + Drift-Intel-engagement-intent per EU AI Act Art. 14)
  4. Drift Audiences account-intent-score (per-account intent-ML-model-version + per-page-visit-event + per-engagement-event + per-firmographic-update-event + Drift-Audiences-cross-tenant-intent-data-isolation per EU AI Act Art. 10 + GDPR Art. 28 + CCPA/CPRA)
  5. Drift Email / Drift Sequences outreach-trigger-decision (per-trigger-decision + Drift-Email-cadence-id + per-template-version + per-rep-approval-toggle + Drift-Video-recording-isolation per SOC 2 CC7.2 + EU AI Act Art. 12 + Art. 14)
  6. Drift-Salesloft-cross-product-tenant-mapping → Salesforce / HubSpot / 6sense / ZoomInfo / Pardot / Marketo write-back (pre-image + post-image + Drift-bot-conversation-id + Salesloft-cadence-id + lead-id + meeting-uuid + decision-timestamp per SOC 2 CC7.2 + GDPR Art. 17 + CCPA/CPRA deletion-propagation)

What to capture in the Drift audit checklist

For each conversation-event logged by Drift AI Site Concierge + Drift Prospector + Drift Fastlane + Drift Audiences + Drift Email + Drift Video + Drift Intel, an auditor will want to see a 14-column per-conversation-AI-reply-decision provenance join-table with the following columns: per-conversation-uuid, per-tenant-id, per-bot-version, per-prompt-text, per-reply-text, per-knowledge-base-doc-id, per-cited-source-doc-id, per-rep-approval-toggle, per-confidence-threshold, per-decision-timestamp, per-source-system-write, per-downstream-state-change, per-EU-caller-consent-bit, per-GDPR-DSAR-propagation-flag. Without this join-table, Drift cannot reconstruct any per-conversation AI-decision to the level SOC 2 CC7.2 + EU AI Act Art. 12 + Art. 14 expect.

The 14-column per-conversation-AI-reply-decision provenance join-table is the single most important evidence artifact your Drift audit will produce. If you cannot produce it today, that is the audit gap to close first.

The per-lead-AI-routing-decision provenance join-table

Drift Prospector makes a routing-decision every time an inbound Drift-bot conversation crosses a threshold (lead-fit-score, ICP-match, intent-score, page-visit-depth, account-tier). Each per-lead-decision needs a 16-column join-table with: per-lead-id, per-tenant-id, per-Drift-Prospector-ML-model-version, per-lead-fit-score, per-ICP-match-percentage, per-Drift-Audiences-account-intent-score, per-rep-eligibility-set, per-rep-availability-snapshot, per-timezone-overlay, per-account-tier, per-lead-source, per-GDPR-consent-bit, per-EU-caller-consent-bit, per-CCPA-CPRA-consent-bit, per-decision-timestamp, per-downstream-CRM-write-target-id. Without this table, a GDPR Art. 22 DSAR cannot reconstruct why a particular EU-buyer was routed to a particular SDR — which is a guaranteed Article 22 enforcement trigger.

Cross-tenant Drift-bot-isolation-evidence

Drift's multi-tenant bot-platform shares a single Drift-AI-reply-model + Drift-Audiences-account-intent-model + Drift-Site-Concierge-knowledge-base + Drift-Video-recording-store + Drift-Intel-engagement-data-store across 7,000+ tenants. The auditor will want to see an 18-row cross-tenant Drift-bot-isolation-evidence table with: per-tenant CMK, per-tenant OAuth-scope-boundary, per-tenant Drift-bot-conversation-visibility-rule, per-tenant Drift-Audiences-intent-data-isolation, per-tenant Drift-Fastlane-meeting-rule-isolation, per-tenant Drift-Video-recording-isolation, per-tenant Drift-Intel-engagement-data-isolation, per-tenant Drift-Email-cadence-isolation, per-tenant Drift-Audiences-cross-channel-isolation, per-tenant Drift-Prospector-ML-model-isolation, per-tenant Drift-Email-cadence-template-isolation, per-tenant Drift-Fastlane-Schedule-template-isolation, per-tenant Drift-Video-recording-storage-isolation, per-tenant Drift-Intel-engagement-event-isolation, per-tenant Drift-Salesloft-cross-product-tenant-mapping, per-tenant Drift-Audiences-account-tier-isolation, per-tenant Drift-conversation-history-isolation, per-tenant Drift-Audiences-3rd-party-data-sharing-control. This is the table that survives SOC 2 CC6.1 + GDPR Art. 28 + EU AI Act Art. 10 audits.

The Drift-Salesloft-cross-product-tenant-mapping question

Post-merger, every Drift-tenant must be mapped to a single Salesloft-tenant-id, with explicit documentation of: (a) how Drift-bot-conversation-events flow into Salesloft-cadence-step-record (b) how Drift-Video-recordings get tagged with Salesloft-cadence-id (c) how Drift-Intel-engagement-events flow into Salesloft-Reports (d) how Drift-Audiences-account-intent-scores flow into Salesloft-Drift-Prospector-ML-training-set (e) how Drift-AI-reply-decisions get attributed to Salesloft-SDR or Drift-rep (f) cross-product deletion-propagation when a tenant exercises GDPR Art. 17 or CCPA/CPRA deletion. The 22-row Drift-Salesloft-cross-product-tenant-mapping is the single artifact that satisfies SOC 2 CC7.2 + GDPR Art. 17 + EU AI Act Art. 10 + CCPA/CPRA deletion-propagation simultaneously.
