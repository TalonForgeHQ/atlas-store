21-Question Buyer Checklist (cross-vendor)

  1. Can the vendor produce a per-MIC-row-id attestation report covering all production MICs in use?
  2. Can the vendor produce per-MIC-firmware-version reports covering firmware versions deployed over the last 12 months?
  3. Can the vendor produce per-build-toolchain-sha reports linking each firmware build to a specific GCC/LLVM commit hash?
  4. Can the vendor produce per-job-id + per-kernel-id + per-kernel-build-id reports for the last 90 days?
  5. Can the vendor produce per-rack-serial + per-rack-firmware-rev attestation reports for all production racks?
  6. Can the vendor produce per-MIC-secure-boot-counter reports showing no firmware rollback events?
  7. For Tenstorrent: per-MIC-tensix-row-id + per-tensix-program-id attestation?
  8. For SambaNova: per-RDU-program-id + per-RDU-pattern-memory-id + per-CoE-expert-id attestation?
  9. For SambaNova: per-CoE-routing-decision-id audit trail for the last 12 months?
  10. For Lightmatter: per-photon-id + per-MZI-id + per-MZI-tuning-version attestation?
  11. For Lightmatter: per-waveguide-id + per-waveguide-routing-id + per-optical-loss-budget routing audit?
  12. Does the vendor have SOC 2 Type II attestation covering the audit-trail surface?
  13. Does the vendor have a GDPR DPA covering EU customer data processed by the audit-trail surface?
  14. Does the vendor have ISO 27001 + ISO 42001 certification?
  15. Does the vendor have EU AI Act readiness documentation covering Art. 12 (record-keeping) + Art. 53(d) (GPAI training-data-summary)?
  16. Does the vendor have a FedRAMP Moderate authorization or in-process roadmap?
  17. Does the vendor have a CCPA/CPRA + state-privacy-law compliance program?
  18. Does the vendor have a supply-chain attestation program covering silicon foundry + packaging + assembly?
  19. Does the vendor have a hardware-tamper-evidence program covering shipping + receiving + rack installation?
  20. Does the vendor have a WORM-archive policy for audit-trail data (6+ year retention)?
  21. Does the vendor have a quarterly-reconstruction-drill program to verify audit-trail completeness?
