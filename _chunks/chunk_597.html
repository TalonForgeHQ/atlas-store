EU AI Act Aug 2 2026 layer on top of Scrut's framework library

For AI-vendor procurement under the EU AI Act high-risk-system controls, the existing SOC 2 + ISO 27001 + ISO 27701 evidence binder is the floor. The AI-specific layer adds five audit-trails: (1) per-system risk-tier classification, (2) per-data-category provenance, (3) per-model + per-prompt + per-tool-version manifest, (4) per-incident + per-human-override log, (5) per-output + per-evaluation evidence pack. India DPDP Act 2023 enforcement Q3 2026

  • Consent manager: record consent receipt + withdrawal timestamp + processing-purpose at each collection point.

  • Breach notification: CERT-In 6-hour incident-notification SLA for certain incident classes.
  • Cross-border transfer: government-notification + standard-contractual-clause + country-whitelist evidence.
  • Children's data: verifiable parental-consent + processing-restriction evidence for under-18 data principals.
