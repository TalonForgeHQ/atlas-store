What "OpsAI" actually is — the AI product surface

OpsAI is BuildOps's first-party AI product family. The verbatim first-party /platform/opsai/ meta-description captures the four product capabilities in one sentence:

"OpsAI recaps visits, scans POs, flags risks, and gives every role a clear next step, right where the work happens."

Each capability maps to a discrete audit-letter column:

  • Recaps visits → opsai_recap_visit_id column — per-visit recap record linked to source visit_id
  • Scans POs → po_scan_id + po_id columns — per-PO scan result linked to source PO
  • Flags risks → risk_flag_id + risk_category columns — per-risk-flag record with category taxonomy
  • Gives every role a clear next step → next_step_id column — per-recommendation record linked to the OpsAI role context (PM / foreman / dispatcher / executive)

OpsAI is the only cohort member that ships an AI product with a four-action surface (recaps / scans / flags / next-step) — ServiceTitan AI Suite has 7 discrete features and Jobber AI Receptionist has 1. OpsAI sits between: more architectural than AI Receptionist, narrower than AI Suite, but specifically trades-aware (visit-recap + PO-scan + risk-flag are all trades-native signals).
