Why this ledger matters for AI Agent builders

Most AI agent builders trust an eval framework that gives them a passing score on a small held-out set. The procurement question is: when the agent ships, can you re-run that same scoring rubric against the production-traffic trace, with a regression baseline pinned and a regression alert paged? That's a different question than "did the eval pass in dev." Braintrust's Experiment Tracking + AI Model Evaluation + A/B Testing primitives are designed to answer it — but only if the receipt design captures the join.
