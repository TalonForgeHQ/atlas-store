Bigeye vs Cleanlab vs Monte Carlo: Data Quality & Observability Comparison 2026

Bigeye is the auto-generated-column-lineage data observability platform. Cleanlab is the data-centric-AI label-noise-detection + dataset-validation + trustworthy-LLM-score platform. Monte Carlo is the data-reliability freshness + volume + schema + end-to-end-lineage platform. This is the audit-trail join-table comparison for SOC 2 CC7.2 + EU AI Act Art. 12 + ISO 42001 9.4 + GDPR Art. 28 + OWASP ML Top 10 + OWASP LLM Top 10 reasoning-chain reconstruction.

Quick verdict

If you ship a dbt + Airflow + Snowflake + Databricks stack and need auto-generated-column-lineage with monitor + test coverage, pick Bigeye. If you're building ML/LLM models and need label-noise-detection + dataset-validation + Trustworthy-Language-Model-Score at training time, pick Cleanlab. If you need end-to-end data-reliability across the modern data stack with freshness + volume + schema + lineage for analytics teams, pick Monte Carlo. Most teams ship Bigeye or Monte Carlo for production-data-monitoring, then layer Cleanlab for ML-data-quality + label-noise + OOD-detection.
