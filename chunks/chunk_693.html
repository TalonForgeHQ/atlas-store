The Five Audit Questions

1. One Contract Request Receipt Across Intake, Ironflow, and AI Agents

Can a single contract_request_receipt reconstruct the original intake (requestor, business unit, contract type, counterparty, deal value bracket, jurisdiction), the per-step Ironflow execution (step ordering, step policy threshold, attorney/supervisor reviewer, fallback escalation, due-date SLA), the AI Agent's per-action output with rule and clause citations, the counterparty signature receipt, the downstream Salesforce Opportunity + NetSuite Order Form state change with timestamp and actor, and the post-execution analytics dashboard refresh? This is the core EU AI Act Article 12, ISO/IEC 42001, NIST AI RMF 600-1, and SOC 2 CC7.2 evidence chain.

2. Deployment-Mode Isolation Evidence Across Cloud, Private, and Public Cloud

Ironclad runs three deployment modes — Ironclad Cloud multi-tenant AWS, Ironclad Private hosted in customer-VPC, and Ironclad Public Cloud perpetual customer-isolated — and each mode needs its own isolation evidence. The auditable artifact should join deployment_mode, AWS region, customer VPC ID (for Private), KMS key version, S3/EC2 encryption state, S3 Object Lock retention, Ironclad AI Agent GPU node isolation, Ironclad Business Intelligence warehouse schema isolation, Ironclad Repository counterparty-DB segregation, monthly restore test, deletion receipt, and Schrems II SCC posture for EU-tenant egress.

3. Clause-Level Provenance for AI Assist and AI Agent Recommendations

AI Assist and AI Agents both surface clause recommendations, fallback positions, and fallback-by-counterparty. For every changed clause, the evidence packet should preserve AI Agent version, prompt and policy version, fallback position rank, internal benchmark reference, source clause library hash, counterparty redline diff, attorney override, accepted redline, Clickwrap + e-signature binding record (DocuSign + Adobe Sign), and final signed-clause hash. Without that chain, an in-house counsel cannot prove why a standard MSA term moved from "Mutual Indemnification" to "Vendor Indemnification with cap."

4. Licensed-Professional Human Oversight Across Every Step

Every material Ironflow step and every AI Agent action should carry the supervisory lawyer, jurisdiction of bar admission, ABA Model Rule 1.1 technology-competence control, Model Rule 1.6 confidentiality and privilege classification, Model Rule 5.5 unauthorized-practice guardrail result, applicable UK SRA or EU Member State Bar rule, applicable eIDAS QES or UETA acceptance hash, and the time-stamped human acceptance or rejection rationale. This converts "human in the loop" into an EU AI Act Article 14(4) machine-readable record.

5. Counterparty PII Redaction + Downstream State-Change Defense

The Ironclad Repository holds counterparty names, emails, beneficial-ownership data, payment instructions, and counterparty-bankruptcy flags. A defensible audit pack should join counterparty-record hash, PII redaction pipeline stage, content_classification label, attorney-account access grant, AI Access gateway key-version, downstream Salesforce Opportunity + NetSuite + Coupa + Workato state-change event, and downstream-failure rollback receipt under ABA Model Rule 5.3 supervision-of-nonlawyer-assistants. Without that join-table, an auditor cannot separate Ironclad's layer from the operational layer it triggers.
