Gap 2 — Framework-Inheritance + Overlap-Elimination Rule Provenance

Across the 25+ frameworks (SOC 2 + ISO 27001 + ISO 27701 + ISO 42001 + HIPAA + GDPR + PCI DSS + NIST CSF + NIST 800-53 + CMMC + EU AI Act + HITRUST + SOX 404), can Secureframe export the per-framework-inheritance-rule version, per-overlap-elimination-decision version, per-customer-visible-overlap-report version, and per-framework-overlap-rule-change-event with timestamp + approver?

The framework-inheritance + overlap-elimination primitive is the SMB-PMF-first cohort-differentiator no other cycle-2 sibling publishes by default; without it, a customer cannot prove why a single control was accepted across SOC 2 + ISO 27001 + ISO 42001 without re-collection.

Gap 3 — Evidence-to-Auditor + Auditor-Portal Lineage

For every auditor accessing a customer's tenant via the Auditor Portal, can Secureframe export the per-auditor-access-event, per-NDA-gated-access-receipt, per-confidentiality-binding-form version, per-auditor-portal-export-event, per-control-evidence-walkthrough-version, and per-auditor-disagreement-event + resolution?

When the same SOC 2 Type II period is re-issued under a new auditor, the receipt should identify which evidence + which decisions were preserved, re-performed, or invalidated rather than silently updating the SOC 2 score.

Gap 4 — Policy-Template + Policy-Acknowledgement + Policy-Exception Chain

For every customer-employee policy acknowledgement, can Secureframe export the policy-template version, policy-publish-event, per-employee-acknowledgement-event + acknowledgement-receipt + IP-address + user-agent + timestamp, per-policy-exception-event + exception-rationale-version + exception-approver + exception-expiry, and per-policy-supersede-event?

The cohort-differentiator is whether the exception rationale is itself versioned and linked to a risk-register entry. If not, a customer cannot prove why a specific employee was exempted from a specific policy at a specific time without manual evidence-gathering.

Gap 5 — Trust Center Publishing + Questionnaire-Automation Customer-Facing Surface
