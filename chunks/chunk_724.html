The Five Audit Gaps

Gap 1 — Close-Period Source-System Version Pinning

For every close-period reconciliation packet, journal-entry preparation, compliance task, and Accounting-AI suggestion, can FloQast export the (source-system, source-version, extraction-time, FloQast close-task version, Accounting-AI model version) tuple that produced it — durable across GL / sub-ledger / ERP / bank-API source changes?

Without this tuple, a controller cannot reconstruct why a specific reconciliation packet looked the way it did on a specific close-period day after the underlying GL changed.

Gap 2 — Accounting-AI Version + Suggestion Lineage

For every AI-suggested reconciliation, journal entry, flux analysis, ASC 606 step, or lease-accounting classification, can FloQast replay the Accounting-AI prompt, the model version, the suggestion confidence, the LLM sub-processor version (OpenAI / Anthropic / Google), the reviewer-accountant acceptance or override, and the resulting downstream close-task?

This is the cohort-differentiator. The "Ethical, Auditable AI Impact" pillar requires more than confidence-score display — it requires per-suggestion exportability for SOX 404, AICPA SOC 2 Type II, ISO/IEC 42001 (AIMS), and EU AI Act Art. 9 + 14(4) + 50 + 53(1)(b).

Gap 3 — Reviewer-Accountant Cascade + Approval-Event Lineage

For every close-task handoff, AI-suggestion override, journal-entry approval, and audit-package sign-off, can FloQast export who/when/role/region/SSO/MFA — and reconstruct the pre-override vs post-override artifact pair so the controller's reviewer-accountant decisions are auditable downstream?

Required by SOX 404 + AICPA SOC 2 CC6.1 + ISO 27001 A.9.4 segregation-of-duties. The CPA-auditor founder pedigree raises the procurement confidence bar on this gap specifically.

Gap 4 — Tenant Isolation + Per-Region Data-Residency for the Audit Package

When the customer's external auditor requests the audit-ready package, can FloQast prove that only the right tenant's source data, Accounting-AI suggestions, overrides, and approvals were visible — across GL, reconciliations, journal entries, AI scope, encryption keys, KMS, and region?

Required by SOX 404, SEC Reg S-K Item 106, Schrems II SCC, EU-US DPF, UK Addendum, CCPA/CPRA, APPI, and GDPR Art. 28.

Gap 5 — Per-Audit-Package Exportable Receipt Across GAAP / IFRS / SOX / SOC 2 / ISO 27001 / ISO/IEC 42001

Can FloQast ship a single export that ties the customer's most recent audit-package to the version history of every source-system, Accounting-AI model, reviewer-accountant, and approval-event that produced it — durable across regulatory close cycles and auditor re-creation requests?

This is the cohort-closing primitive. The CPA-auditor founder + "Ethical, Auditable AI Impact" pillar + SOC 1 / SOC 2 / SOC 3 + ISO 27001 compliance posture imply the receipt should exist by design, not by exception.
