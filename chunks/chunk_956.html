The five-question evidence-gap checklist

  1. Security Rating lineage replay. Can a chosen Bitsight Security Rating update be traced back through its scan, finding vector, severity, remediation ticket, Trust Management Hub cohort, tenant, and SCF control mapping? The export should bind stable identifiers such as rating_id, scan_id, finding_id, vector, severity, remediation_ticket_id, trust_hub_cohort_id, tenant_id, scf_control_id, and scf_control_version.
  2. Tenant + Trust Management Hub cohort isolation. Can Bitsight demonstrate that one customer's Security Ratings, Vulnerability Intelligence, Attack Surface Intelligence, Adversary & Ransomware Intelligence, and remediation tickets never cross into another tenant through a shared cohort, shared TRACE intake, or shared dark-web-intelligence ingestion? The evidence should pair tenant boundaries with Trust Management Hub cohort partitioning, TRACE submission scoping, integration consent, credential scope, and deletion records.
  3. Dark Web Intelligence for Supply Chains audit trail. Every Dark Web Intelligence for Supply Chains ingest event needs a per-event-id, per-source-feed, per-detection-rule-version, per-credential-classification, per-replay-window, and per-tenant-isolation record. EU AI Act Art. 50(1) requires AI-generation disclosure for every EU-resident Dark Web Intelligence signal; ISO/IEC 42001:2023 AIMS clause 8.4 requires per-AI-action operational records.
  4. SEC cyber 8-K Item 1.05 replay. For SEC registrants using Bitsight Security Ratings as their TPRM data source, can a Security-Rating-discovered material cybersecurity incident be replayed within the Reg-FD 4-business-day window? The replay must include the scan timestamp, rating delta, vector severity, supply-chain-exposure chain, TRACE escalation, internal escalation chain, and disclosure-ready artifact.
  5. NIS2 Art. 21 + ISO/IEC 42001 + AWS compliance posture. NIS2 Art. 21(2)(d) vulnerability-handling and Art. 21(2)(e) signed-firmware require per-finding handling-state and per-integration signed-firmware receipts.
