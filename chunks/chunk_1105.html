Five failure modes the audit will catch in 2026

  1. "EU-sovereign" without EU incorporation. The vendor is a Delaware C-Corp with a Berlin mailing address. Check the LEI + HRB + VAT + managing directors.
  2. "ISO 27001 certified" without a certificate. The vendor claims "ISO 27001 in progress" or "self-attested." Demand the first-party certificate PDF + certifying body + audit date.
  3. "DORA aligned" without a DORA Addendum. The vendor has a marketing page but no signed DORA Addendum with audit + exit + step-in rights. Walk away.
  4. "EU hosting" with US telemetry breach. The application servers sit in Frankfurt, but the analytics + product-improvement telemetry leaves the EU. Sub-processor cascade review is the only way to detect this.
  5. "AI agent" with no human-in-the-loop receipt. The agent takes high-risk actions autonomously with no `ai_handoff_id` + `ai_handoff_reason` + `human_override_id`. The audit will treat it as fully autonomous and most EU AI Act-aligned buyers will reject it.
