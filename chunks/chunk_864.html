3. The Risk Operations Center (ROC) thesis — Scrut's wedge

Scrut's flagship is the Risk Operations Center (ROC) — a continuous-control-monitoring substrate distinct from Vanta's Trust Management Platform and Drata's dashboard-first Continuous Compliance Automation. The ROC thesis: SOC 2 + ISO 42001 + HIPAA + PCI DSS 4.0 buying is shifting from "evidence-on-demand" (collect 200 evidence items at audit time) to "continuous-control-monitoring" (every control has a live health score, every TPRM vendor has a live risk tier, every policy acknowledgment has a live timestamp).

For mid-market + APAC enterprise buyers who already operate an InfoSec-first posture (existing SOC 2 + ISO 27001 + PCI DSS 4.0 + HIPAA + ISO 42001 stack + internal GRC team), the ROC layer is the differentiator. For enterprise US buyers new to compliance automation, Vanta + Drata + Secureframe remain the default. For buyers with a strong InfoSec-first posture who want continuous-control-monitoring + ROC + mid-market price point + APAC presence — Scrut ships the strongest wedge.

4. SOC 2 + ISO 42001 + HIPAA buying decision criteria

Three questions determine which of the 4 wins the deal:

  1. Are you mid-market + APAC, or enterprise US? Mid-market + APAC → Scrut. Enterprise US → Vanta + Drata + Secureframe.
  2. Do you already have InfoSec-first posture + custom control framework? Yes → Scrut ROC depth matters. No → Vanta + Drata + Secureframe default flows are faster.
  3. Do you have a TPRM Vendor Risk program with sub-processor cascades? All four ship TPRM. Scrut + Vanta have the deepest per-TPRM-vendor-tier SLA cascade.

6. The audit-letter cascade (the part auditors actually read)

At audit time, your SOC 2 + ISO 42001 auditor will request 5 things, in order:

  1. Per-control per-evidence-artifact-sha256 hash chain (immutable, per-quarterly-revalidation).
  2. Per-framework-version pin (SOC 2 2017 vs 2022 vs TSC 2026; ISO 27001:2013 vs 2022; ISO 42001:2023).
  3. Per-integration health score freshness (per-collector-integration-version + last-pull-timestamp).
  4. Per-TPRM-vendor-tier sub-processor cascade (critical-tier vendor SLAs + sub-processor DPA evidence).
  5. Per-auditor-portal sub-processor-cascade with read-only auditor credentials + per-evidence-export retention.

All 4 platforms ship the 5-layer cascade. The differentiation is auditor-portal UX: Scrut's Auditor Portal + Vanta's Auditor Directory + Drata's auditor portal + Secureframe's auditor portal are the read-only auditor surfaces. The one with the cleanest per-evidence-export + per-quarterly-revalidation pin wins the audit-time UX battle.
