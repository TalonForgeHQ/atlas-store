Five questions for procurement

  1. Where in the audit log can a regulated buyer (a) see that Abnormal's per-tenant baseline never contributes to another tenant's model, (b) opt out tenant-by-tenant, and (c) read the confusion-matrix excerpt for their top attack types?
  2. For any AI-Automation action (quarantine / restore / delete / hold), can Abnormal produce the approver identity + role + timestamp + the auto-remediated downstream effect + the rollback target + the per-tenant retention window?
  3. Does Abnormal publish a 14-day customer-notification SLA for sub-processor changes that touch customer-content, and a 24-month log of historical changes?
  4. Can a regulated tenant export Detection-360 decisions in CSV / JSONL / Parquet for at least 365 days, and receive a per-tenant deletion-cascade receipt on cancellation?
  5. Where is Abnormal's public responsible-disclosure / CVE-disclosure timeline, and is there a 12-month incident-severity attestation an enterprise procurement officer can take to audit (including an "no incidents in scope" line)?
