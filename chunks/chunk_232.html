1. The Make surface (what gets audited)

Make.com (formerly Integromat, founded 2012 Prague, rebranded Make 2022 after StepStone Group growth investment) is the canonical visual-no-code-workflow-automation + AI-agent-orchestration platform. The audit surface spans 5 primitives:

  • Scenarios — visual drag-and-drop orchestration graphs (the canonical workflow definition).
  • Modules — the 1000+ Apps catalog + custom HTTP/Webhook/GraphQL modules + the canonical AI-Agent module surface.
  • Bundles — per-execution payloads carrying arrays of records between modules.
  • Executions — Scenario runs, each producing a Sequence of Bundles, retry-able, schedulable.
  • Data-stores — keyed state primitives + CMK/BYOK + per-region flag.
