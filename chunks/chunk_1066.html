Where Helicone fits in the five-vendor gateway cohort

Portkey contributes a managed provider-credential vault and virtual-key control plane. Helicone contributes the OpenAI-compatible reverse-proxy and request/session observability lane. LiteLLM contributes the open-source Python SDK and self-hosted proxy-router lane. Unify contributes GTM-revenue lineage from routed calls to CRM opportunities. Bifrost contributes MCP-tool governance and authorization joined to provider fallback. The comparison prevents a generic “AI gateway” label from hiding materially different operating models.
