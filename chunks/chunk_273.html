3. Vanta — the customer-base + AI-feature leader

Vanta started as the 2017 YC-W19 SOC 2 automation tool, expanded to 100+ frameworks (SOC 2 + ISO 27001 + ISO 27701 + HIPAA + GDPR + PCI DSS + NIST CSF + FedRAMP readiness), and now serves 8,000+ customers with the largest SaaS-market share in compliance automation. The 2026 inflection was Vanta AI + Vanta Questionnaire Automation + Vanta Risk Management + Vanta CRM — Vanta moved from "automate evidence collection" to "automate the entire trust operation." The trust surface worth scrutinizing: any AI-feature inside a SOC 2 pipeline inherits MITRE ATLAS + OWASP LLM Top 10 risk surface (prompt-injection on uploaded vendor security questionnaires, RAG-retrieval-poisoning on the trust-insights feed, multi-modal attack on uploaded evidence PDFs). Buyers I work with in fintech + healthcare ask specifically about the per-control-id → per-policy-id → per-evidence-id → per-Vanta-AI-completion-id join-table — the audit binder row-level join that proves an AI-generated trust claim corresponds to a real control with verifiable evidence. What Vanta does best: largest integration graph (200+), fastest time-to-SOC-2 (~30 days vs AuditBoard's 90+), cleanest questionnaire-automation UX. What to verify at procurement: the AI-feature defensive controls, the per-tenant evidence-WORM-retention story, the FedRAMP-readiness timeline.

4. Drata — the integration-first runner-up

Drata (Tomer Levi + Adam Markowitz, San Diego, 2019) joined Vanta as a top-2 compliance automation vendor at the 2024 Series C and has closed a ~$2B valuation gap. Drata's differentiator is deeper integrations per vendor (Drata pushes 200+ integrations but twice as many Health-Insurance-Payment-Pattern + SOC-specific integration categories) and a more opinionated Risk Management + Continuous Control Monitoring stack. The 2026 AI-feature was Drata AI for control-writeup + risk-scoring, which shifted the trust surface similarly to Vanta. What Drata does best: integration depth, Risk Management + Continuous Control Monitoring (vs Vanta's CRM-bundle), tighter Drata-Pro auditor network. What to verify: the Drata-AI-output-validation pipeline, the per-tenant evidence-WORM-retention freshness under load.

5. Secureframe — the price-aggressive mid-market

Secureframe (Shruti Gupta + Brian Burns, YC-S19, San Francisco) sits between Vanta's enterprise pitch and Drata's integration depth, but wins on aggressive pricing (Secureframe Pro at $10-20K/year vs Vanta's $20-40K) and a UX optimized for "first-time CISO" buyers. The 2026 AI-feature was Secureframe AI, primarily focused on auto-generated policy + control-writeup. What Secureframe does best: aggressive pricing, fast onboarding, simpler frameworks-inheritance model. What to verify: the AI-feature audit-defensibility (Secureframe's AI is newer than Vanta's), the platform-fit as customers scale beyond 200 employees.

6. Hyperproof — the multi-framework-inheritance specialist

Hyperproof (Kris Turscak, founded 2018 Bellevue WA, $9.5M Series A) is the specialist platform for buyers running 6+ frameworks in parallel (SOC 2 + ISO 27001 + ISO 27701 + HIPAA + PCI DSS + FedRAMP + NIST CSF + custom frameworks) and want them inherited from a single evidence spine. Hyperproof's tagline is "compliance operations, not compliance automation." The 2026 AI-feature was Hyperproof AI + multi-framework inheritance engine. What Hyperproof does best: the multi-framework inheritance engine is genuinely different from Vanta's, the per-control evidence-mapping UX is more granular. What to verify: integration breadth (Hyperproof has ~100 integrations vs Vanta's 200+), scale confidence (smaller customer base than the top-3).

7. AuditBoard — the audit-first incumbent

AuditBoard (Scott Arnold, founded 2014 Los Angeles, $100M+ raised, public-company floor) is the audit-first compliance platform. The difference is the audience: AuditBoard's primary users are internal auditors (SOX + SOC 1 + ISO 27001 + CMMC + AS/NZS + CMMI + FFIEC), with IT-GRC features as a secondary layer. The 2026 AI-feature was AuditBoard AIdA + Cross-Product Linking (audit issues ↔ risks ↔ controls). What AuditBoard does best: the most credible auditor-first UX, the largest enterprise IT-GRC footprint, the strongest SOX presence. What to verify: the SaaS-compliance-automation angle (AuditBoard's mid-market launch + IT-GRC positioning is the competitive swap with Vanta + Drata + Secureframe).
