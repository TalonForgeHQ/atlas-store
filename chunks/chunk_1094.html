Five-wedge non-overlap rubric

  1. AI-SRE investigate-agent substrate: Resolve AI's named "investigate" agent that picks up an alert and runs a multi-step investigation is the cohort-unique wedge, distinct from a pure alerting router or a generic observability dashboard.
  2. Kubernetes-aware satellite gateway: the Resolve AI satellite acts as a secure gateway that reads Kubernetes metadata or proxies tool data, with explicit scope configuration (metadata-only vs full-proxy) — a privacy/control lever that a buyer can pin in a SOC 2 evidence row.
  3. Background-agent operational tasks: Resolve AI ships background agents that autonomously run recurring engineering work even when no alert is attached, distinguishing them from on-call-only automation.
  4. Human override + RCA replay: Resolve AI's evidence chain joins the alert to the agent's root-cause analysis, the proposed remediation action, and the human override that approved it — all under one run_id.
  5. Compliance posture for regulated ops: SOC 2 Type II + GDPR + HIPAA, plus SSO + RBAC + service-account token controls, sits as a verifiable subprocess record per Resolve AI run.

Resolve AI versus the alternative choices

Choose Resolve AI when the missing evidence is the end-to-end replay from alert to RCA to remediation with a human override, under SOC 2 Type II / GDPR / HIPAA controls, and where the agent itself touches Kubernetes on the operator's behalf.

Choose traditional AIOps (Moogsoft, BigPanda, ServiceNow ITSM) when the requirement is alert correlation and noise reduction, not autonomous investigation.

Choose observability platforms (Datadog, Honeycomb, Grafana, New Relic) when the requirement is dashboards, tracing, and metrics, not a runnable agent.

Choose on-call fan-out (PagerDuty, Opsgenie, FireHydrant) when the requirement is to page a human responder, not to close the loop.

Choose eval/observability platforms for agents (Braintrust, Langfuse, Confident AI, Arize) when the work is development-time scoring, not production incident response.
