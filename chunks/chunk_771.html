Common evidence failures

  • Showing an AI Scribe summary without the LLM sub-processor invocation, the per-incident human-oversight acceptance/rejection log, the retention window, and the region routing per tenant.
  • Logging an AI-drafted status page update without the Art. 50 transparency-labeling ("AI-drafted, reviewed before publishing") + the human-oversight acceptance/rejection event + the per-tenant tenant-id scoping.
  • Returning an AI Insights pattern-analysis result that crossed per-tenant boundaries (e.g. a tenant A's incident pattern visible to a tenant B's Insights user because the aggregation was scoped too broadly).
  • Firing a per-incident cross-border notification without NIS2 Art. 21(2)(e) chain-of-custody preservation, forensic evidence retention beyond the notification SLA, and per-tenant data-residency pinning.
