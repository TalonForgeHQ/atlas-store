The 5 audit gaps for Vapi

  1. End-to-end per-call provenance join-table — Vapi's call surface crosses Composer + Monitoring + Simulations + fleet mode + telephony + downstream CRM/billing. The 60-column join-table above is the canonical audit-trail surface for SOC 2 CC7.2 + HIPAA §164.312(b) + EU AI Act Art. 12 procurement review.
  2. Composer self-improvement loop lineage — Vapi's Series B post names the self-improvement loop as a core engineering investment ("the agent that picks up the phone tomorrow is reliably better than the one that picked up the phone today"). The per-improvement-run-id + per-training-dataset-id + per-training-data-PHI-redaction-flag triple must be audit-trail-grade for HIPAA §164.514(b) min-necessary + EU AI Act Art. 10 data governance + Art. 53(d) model-card lineage.
  3. Multi-region residency for the global cohort — The same Series B post names builders in India, China, Brazil, France, UAE, Pakistan, Australia. per-call-id + per-deployment-region + per-recording-storage-region + per-cross-border-transfer-sccs-version-US-EU must satisfy Schrems II + GDPR Art. 28 + India DPDP Act 2023 + Brazil LGPD Art. 33 + UAE PDPL.
  4. State-by-state US AI-disclosure (12 states) — Vapi's enterprise customers span all 12 disclosure-active states. per-jurisdiction-detection-id + per-AI-disclosure-script-version-id + per-human-handoff-evidence-id must satisfy CA SB 1001 §3(a)(2) + Colorado SB 24-205 + Illinois AIVIA + Texas + NY + 7 others.
  5. Voice-cloning + telephony-input fraud detection at scale — 1B+ calls/year means the per-voice-cloning-detection-signal-id + per-wire-fraud-detection-signal-id + per-telephony-input-voice-cloning-detection-id surface must be SOC 2 CC7.2 + FTC Voice Cloning Consumer Protection + FBI voice-cloning-fraud guidance-grade at production scale.
