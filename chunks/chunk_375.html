Why Miro is the unique "live-facilitator-time prompt-injection + cluster-taint + Sidekick-action-item provenance" sibling

  1. The "live participant prompt-injection" gap: in Canva + Figma + Sketch + Penpot + Framer + Adobe Express, the AI runs against a single user's prompt in a single-user design canvas. In Miro, the AI runs against *every participant's prompt* in a multi-participant workshop — and any untrusted participant can poison the AI Agent's cluster + summary + mind-map + decision-log + action-item output before any human sees it.
