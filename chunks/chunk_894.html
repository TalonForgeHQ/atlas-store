  12. flaky_test_detection_score — FailFast cross-run statistical stability score.
  13. self_healing_decision_id — if healed: new locator + rationale.
  14. ci_cd_pipeline_id — Jenkins/GitHub Actions/GitLab CI/CircleCI/Bitrise/Azure DevOps pipeline-link.
