The five-question evidence-gap checklist

  1. SailPoint Atlas decision lineage replay. Can a chosen SailPoint Atlas decision be traced back through its AI-agent recommendation, access-request, access-certification, and CIEM-entitlement-revocation lineage? The export should bind stable identifiers such as sailpoint_atlas_decision_id, ai_agent_recommendation_id, identityiq_access_certification_id, ciem_entitlement_revocation_id, non_employee_lifecycle_event_id, access_risk_violation_id, tenant_id, ai_prompt_hash, model_version, and citation_anchor.
  2. Tenant + Non-Employee cohort isolation. Can SailPoint demonstrate that one customer's Identity Security Cloud tenants, IdentityIQ deployments, Non-Employee Risk Management identities, machine identities, and AI-agent governance decisions never cross into another tenant through a shared connector, shared model version, or shared prompt template? The evidence should pair tenant boundaries with cohort partitioning, integration consent, credential scope, deletion records, and per-tenant model-fine-tuning separation.
  3. AI-agent governance audit trail. Every AI-agent governance decision needs a per-action-id, per-prompt-hash, per-model-version, per-citation-anchor, and per-tenant-isolation record. EU AI Act Art. 14 requires human-oversight for high-risk AI systems including AI-driven identity governance decisions; Art. 50(1) requires AI-generation disclosure; ISO/IEC 42001:2023 AIMS clause 8.4 requires per-AI-action operational records.
  4. SEC cyber 8-K Item 1.05 replay. For SEC registrants using SailPoint as their identity-governance data source, can a SailPoint-discovered material cybersecurity incident (machine-identity compromise, AI-agent governance failure, Non-Employee Risk Management breach) be replayed within the Reg-FD 4-business-day window? The replay must include the AI-agent decision timestamp, access-certification state, CIEM-revocation action, Non-Employee lifecycle event, internal escalation chain, and disclosure-ready artifact.
  5. NIS2 Art. 21 + ISO/IEC 42001 coverage. NIS2 Art. 21(2)(d) vulnerability-handling and Art. 21(2)(e) signed-firmware require per-SailPoint-finding handling-state and per-IdentityIQ-connector signed-firmware receipts. NIS2 Art. 23 incident-reporting requires per-incident replay with cross-border notification within 24h/72h windows. ISO/IEC 42001:2023 AIMS clause 6.1.2 requires AI-risk identification at the identity-governance level; clause 9.3 requires management review of IGA AI risk.
