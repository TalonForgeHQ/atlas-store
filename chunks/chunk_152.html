5-audit-gap map (where Hevo customer audits break)

  1. AI-schema-mapping reasoning-chain gap. Most Hevo competitors surface schema-mapping as a black-box suggestion with no reasoning-chain payload. Auditors asking "why did the AI map this column to that destination field?" get a shrug. Hevo's gap: does the AI-schema-mapping emit a per-suggestion reasoning-chain (prompt + retrieved-schema-id + model-id + suggested-mapping + human-override-flag) joinable to the downstream pipeline-run?
  2. Cross-tenant connector-key isolation gap. 150+ source-connector credentials + 10+ destination-connector credentials on a shared control plane is a high-value cross-tenant attack surface. Hevo's gap: do per-credential isolation guarantees (per-tenant-KMS + per-credential-scope + per-rate-limit + per-source-connector-id) show up in the audit-trail joinable per-tenant-key?
  3. HIPAA-PHI cross-pipeline-leakage gap. When a healthcare-customer pipeline ingests PHI from a source MySQL instance, the PHI sits in the warehouse (Snowflake / BigQuery) and then flows back through Hevo Activate to Salesforce / HubSpot. Hevo's gap: is the per-tenant-PHI-flag + per-PHI-region-pinning + per-PHI-cross-border-transfer-block surfaced as a continuous joinable record, or does the auditor have to reconstruct it from a side-channel (BAA + manual review)?
  4. Schema-drift-event auditability gap. Automated-schema-drift-handling is convenient but a silent audit hazard: if Hevo auto-remaps a column that the customer's compliance team had explicitly excluded, the audit-trail must show the auto-mapping-suggestion-id + human-override-flag + reviewer-id + timestamp. Hevo's gap: is the per-schema-drift-event joinable across the per-pipeline-id + per-old-schema-id + per-new-schema-id + per-reviewer-id chain?
  5. Aug 2026 EU AI Act GPAI enforcement gap. The Aug 2 2026 EU AI Act GPAI Code of Practice enforcement window is 13 days out from this audit. Hevo's AI-schema-mapping + AI-pipeline-config-edit features will be in scope as GPAI-adjacent high-risk AI surfaces. Hevo's gap: is the per-AI-suggestion provenance + per-training-data-transparency + per-fine-tune-job-id + per-Aug-2026-GPAI-Article-53-record joinable per-tenant-key + per-suggestion-id?
