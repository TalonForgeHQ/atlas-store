The 16-column touchpoint-to-MMM audit receipt

  1. tenant_id — owning DTC brand or agency workspace.
  2. touchpoint_id — canonical event identity.
  3. ad_platform_id — Meta / Google / TikTok / Microsoft / LinkedIn / Pinterest / Snapchat.
  4. ad_account_id — owning ad-account scope.
  5. campaign_id and ad_group_id — campaign and ad-set identity.
  6. creative_id — creative-version hash.
  7. click_id — Meta click_id / Google gclid / TikTok ttclid / Microsoft msclkid / LinkedIn li_fat_id.
  8. device_id_hash — first-party deterministic-view device graph identity.
  9. email_hash — resolved identity after Klaviyo / Recharge / Attentive / Postscript / Shopify-customer match.
  10. order_id and gross_revenue / net_revenue — closed-loop order identity.
  11. attribution_model_version — MTA vs MMM+ vs Apex reconciliation handle.
  12. MMM_channel_coefficient and MMM_run_id — media mix modeling coefficient + retraining-timestamp handle.
  13. Apex_incrementality_lift — Apex geo-holdout incremental lift per channel.
  14. cross_tenant_no_bleed_proof — agency 50-brand isolation invariant.

Gap 1 — touchpoint to resolved identity

Every ad-platform click must be joinable to a deterministic-view device-id and, where the customer has consented, to an email-hash, so a reviewer can replay the per-touchpoint-to-order cascade without third-party-pixel loss. Northbeam's first-party Clicks + Deterministic Views lane is the cohort-unique post-IDFA primitive.

Gap 2 — MTA vs MMM+ vs Apex reconciliation

Finance and marketing teams reconcile an audit-letter dispute by joining per-order-id + per-attribution-run-id across MTA, MMM+, and Apex so the same revenue row carries the same answer across the three attribution layers.

Gap 3 — agency multi-tenant isolation

An agency managing 50 DTC brands on Northbeam must show per-tenant credential scoping + per-data-source credential scoping + per-tenant residue cleanup + completion of deletion + per-tenant attribution-run isolation, so brand A's order data cannot leak into brand B's MMM coefficient or Apex lift.

Gap 4 — first-party compliance evidence

/data-security, /gdpr, /privacy, /cookie-policy, and /terms-conditions are all live first-party pages and should be joined to per-ad-platform DPA + per-DTC-tenant sub-processor list + per-region-of-processing (US-East / EU-Frankfurt / APAC-Sydney) for the next enterprise procurement cycle.

Gap 5 — reproducible audit-replay

Per-creative-performance, per-campaign-budget-pacing, per-MMM-incrementality-lift, and per-Apex-holdout-run should be exportable to one CSV/JSONL so a Big-4 auditor can re-run the cascade from raw click to attributed revenue without contacting Northbeam support.
