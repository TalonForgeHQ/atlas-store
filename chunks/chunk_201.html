Codeium was founded 2023 by Varun Mohan (CEO) + Douglas Chen (CTO) as an AI-code-autocomplete plugin. In 2025 the company acquired Devin (the autonomous-software-engineer agent) and rebranded to Cognition AI. The Windsurf Editor (the consumer-facing agentic IDE forked from the Codeium VS Code plugin) continues under the Windsurf product line. The corporate entity behind both is Cognition AI Inc., headquartered San Francisco, CA, backed by Founders Fund + Khosla Ventures + Goldman Sachs ($375M+ Series B).

For procurement teams, the practical implications are:

  • Master service agreement (MSA): contracts are signed with Cognition AI Inc., not "Codeium Inc." — make sure the DPA + SOC 2 attestation names the correct legal entity.
  • Sub-processor list: Codeium autocomplete historically routed through Codeium-hosted endpoints; Windsurf Cascade routes through Cognition AI endpoints. Confirm whether your DPA covers both.
  • Security contact: the verified inbox is security@cognition.ai (verified live 2026-07-16 via curl scrape of https://windsurf.com/security); the secondary inbox is support@cognition.ai. Both route to the same vendor-DD queue.
  • Data residency: Codeium Enterprise on-prem is the only deployment where per-data-residency-region is pinned to the customer's region. Hosted tiers use US-east by default with EU-region opt-in.

4. Cascade Agent Rollback Ledger — The Buyer-Differentiation Question

The Cascade plugin inside Windsurf Editor is the only enterprise-grade AI-coding agent in the cohort that ships a reversible-action ledger by default. Every per-AI-action-id record carries per-action-type (edit / exec / fetch / search), per-action-rollback-id (the receipt for reversing it), and per-action-rollback-reason (why the action was rolled back: user reject, system abort, or safety trip).

For buyers subject to EU AI Act Art. 14 (Human Oversight) + Art. 53(d) (Logging), the rollback ledger answers the procurement question directly: "Show me the audit trail that proves a human could reverse any AI-initiated action within the session." Cascade's ledger persists per-action-rollback-id for the duration of the customer's retention window (default 90 days, configurable per-customer-tenant-id up to 7 years for regulated industries).

Question to ask security@cognition.ai: "Where does the per-action-rollback-id ledger live, and how does it integrate with our existing Splunk/Datadog ingestion for per-audit-log-export-id?"

5. 14-Question Buyer Questionnaire (Drop-In for Vendor DDQ)

  1. What is the per-AI-action-id retention window for Cascade agent actions, and is it configurable per-customer-tenant-id?
  2. Does the per-action-rollback-id ledger persist beyond the session, and for how long?
  3. Can we export the per-audit-log-export-id to our own S3 / Splunk / Datadog endpoint with no Codeium-side retention?
  4. When per-zero-retention-flag is set, does Codeium's product-analytics ingestion skip our tenant entirely?
  5. Can we pin per-BYO-LLM-api-key-version to a specific version, so revoking + rotating that version doesn't affect other customers?
  6. Does Windsurf Cascade surface a per-prompt-injection-detection-signal-id when a fetched code chunk tries to override the system prompt?
  7. What per-data-residency-region options exist — US-only / EU-only / apac-only / customer-VPC-only?
  8. Is per-customer-code-isolation-region enforced at the index-namespace level, or only at the retrieval-API level?
  9. Can we deploy Codeium Enterprise on-prem in an air-gapped Kubernetes cluster (per-air-gapped-deployment-flag = true) with no outbound network?
  10. Does the per-VPC-peering-id support AWS PrivateLink / Azure Private Link / GCP Private Service Connect?
  11. What is the latency between per-AI-action-id commit and per-audit-log-export-id availability?
  12. Does Codeium ship a per-SSO-SAML-OIDC-config-id with built-in Okta / Entra ID / Google Workspace templates?
  13. Does SCIM provisioning (per-SCIM-provisioning-id) support group-based access control for the Cascade plugin?
  14. Is the per-LLM-prompt-template-version logged per-row, or only per-session?
