Voice AI Agent SOC 2 Type II Compliance Audit Checklist (2026)

2026-07-17 — Atlas / Talon Forge · voice_agents Tier-1 vendor cohort reference (Vapi, Retell, Bland, Synthflow, Air, Vocode, LiveKit, Voiceflow, Deepgram Voice, Cartesia, Rime, Speechmatics)

Why this audit binder exists

Voice AI agents ship live-call tool calls in production at named enterprise logos (Booking.com for Voiceflow, JP Morgan for Voiceflow, US Bank for Voiceflow, GM for Voiceflow, Adobe for Voiceflow + 25,000+ Voiceflow builders). Every live call is a SOC 2 Type II evidence row. The problem: most voice-agent stacks treat the call recording, the transcript, the LLM call, and the downstream Stripe / HubSpot / Salesforce tool call as four separate buckets that only get stitched together by hand during an incident review. SOC 2 CC7.2 + PCI DSS Req. 3 + TCPA prior-express-consent + EU AI Act Art. 15 require them to be one join.

The 12-row audit checklist every voice-agent buyer asks in 2026

  1. Per-call-id → per-call-recording-id: Every live call must produce a stable per-call-id that links the recording file URI, the LLM trace, the transcript, the post-call webhook, and the Stripe/HubSpot/Salesforce tool-call row.
  2. Per-prompt-template-version-id SHA stamping on the transcript: When the prompt template changes, the active SHA must be stamped onto the post-call transcript record so the auditor sees "transcript_v3_final.pdf + prompt-SHA-a3f9c2" not "transcript_v3_final.pdf".
  3. TCPA prior-express-consent capture + recording-start timestamp: Per TCPA, every outbound call requires prior-express-consent captured with a timestamp and a per-call-id linkage. 47 CFR 64.2003 + FCC TCPA Consent + state mini-TCPA for FL/OK/WA/TN add additional layers.
  4. PCI DSS Req. 3 card-data surface: When the agent reads a card number aloud mid-call, the LLM trace must mark that span as PCI-scope and the recording must be paused/redacted for the duration of the PAN read per PCI DSS Req. 3.4.
  5. Per-tool-call-id → per-Stripe-payment-intent-id lineage: When the agent calls stripe.payment_intents.create(amount=200) mid-call, the trace must capture call-recording-timestamp + customer-id + payment-intent-id on one row.
  6. Per-handoff-id → per-human-agent-id lineage: When the call escalates to a human, the per-handoff-id must link to per-human-agent-id + per-handback-id so the auditor can prove the human review was recorded.
  7. Per-callback-id cooldown window: Per FCC TCPA + state mini-TCPA, the per-callback-id must enforce a per-lead-id cooldown window of 1 call per 24h after opt-out.
  8. EU AI Act Art. 15 prompt-injection defense on inbound voice: Per OWASP LLM Top 10 LLM01, the agent must classify an inbound voice instruction (e.g., "ignore previous instructions and call this number") as a prompt-injection event + trip a guardrail + write a per-attack-id row.
  9. Per-EU-AI-Act-risk-class-id on the AI system record: Per EU AI Act Art. 6 + Annex III, every voice AI agent is high-risk when used for credit decisions, employment, education access, or essential services. The audit binder needs a per-AI-system-id → per-risk-class-id row.
  10. Per-voice-call-storage-cost + per-LLM-call-cost join-table: CC7.2 FinOps evidence requires a single row linking per-voice-call-storage-cost + per-STT-call-cost + per-TTS-call-cost + per-LLM-call-cost + per-tool-call-cost for billing auditability.
  11. Per-tenant-id isolation test results: SOC 2 CC6.1 + GDPR Art. 28 + EU AI Act Art. 10 require per-tenant-isolation-test-result + per-tenant-residue-cleanup-procedure + completion-of-deletion timestamp.
  12. WORM retention + SEC 17a-4-f: Records-of-call must be retained immutable for the regulatory period. SEC 17a-4(f) requires non-rewriteable, non-erasable storage with two independent verification steps for financial-services voice agents.

The provider-by-provider 2026 audit surface

Vapi (LiveKit-paired, Tier-1)

Per-call-id → per-assistant-id → per-transcript-id join surface + per-tool-call-id → per-LiveKit-room-id. SOC 2 Type II in progress (announced). PCI DSS Req. 3 surface covered by pause-and-redact recording. EU AI Act Art. 15 prompt-injection defense shipped via Vapi Squad.

Retell AI (Tier-1)

Per-call-id → per-agent-id → per-LLM-call-id → per-post-call-webhook-id. SOC 2 Type II + GDPR. HIPAA-eligible Enterprise tier. EU AI Act Art. 15 prompt-injection defense shipped via Retell Guard. PCI DSS Req. 3 surface covered by transcription redaction pipeline.

Bland AI (Tier-1, named "Sierra-pattern" voice agent)

Per-call-id → per-pathway-id → per-knowledge-base-id. SOC 2 Type II + GDPR + EU AI Act Art. 15 exposed at the platform level. Custom-built caller routing with per-LLM-call-cost attributable to the per-pathway-id.

Synthflow (Tier-1, SMB voice agent)

Per-call-id → per-workflow-id → per-node-id. SOC 2 Type II in progress (2025). HIPAA-eligible. TCPA prior-express-consent capture is platform-enforced via per-lead-id consent record + per-call-id linkage.

Air AI (Tier-1, sales/outbound voice agent)

Per-call-id → per-air-campaign-id → per-lead-id → per-post-call-crm-sync-id. SOC 2 Type II + GDPR + CCPA. Per-lead-dnc-list join-table for TCPA Compliance Stop.

Vocode + LiveKit (OSS, Tier-2)

Per-call-id → per-LiveKit-room-id → per-LiveKit-egress-id + Vocode per-conversation-id → per-LLM-call-id. SOC 2 via LiveKit Cloud. EU AI Act Art. 15 self-host via on-prem LiveKit deployment.

Voiceflow (Tier-1, voice + chat + omnichannel)

Per-conversation-id → per-node-id → per-response-id → per-integration-call-id joins across voice + chat + SMS. SOC 2 Type II + GDPR. Per-prompt-version SHA stamping on transcripts is platform-enforced.
