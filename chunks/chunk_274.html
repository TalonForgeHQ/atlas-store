Gap 4 — Cross-tenant isolation evidence

SOC 2 CC6.1 and GDPR Art. 28 both require evidence that tenant A's conversations cannot leak into tenant B's copilot context, embeddings, or vector store. The minimum evidence package: per-tenant-id, per-workspace-id, per-copilot-id, per-tenant-residue-cleanup-procedure, completion-of-deletion timestamp, per-conversation-WORM-retention-evidence, per-customer-PII-no-leakage-evidence, per-cross-border-transfer-sccs-version, per-cross-border-transfer-data-flow-country-chain.

Gap 5 — WORM retention + cost attribution
