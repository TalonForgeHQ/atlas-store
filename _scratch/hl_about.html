Securing the Future of AI

At HiddenLayer, we believe AI’s success depends on security. Founded by security researchers who uncovered the first-ever AI-specific malware, we’ve built a company dedicated to protecting the next era of intelligent systems. Today, we partner with the world’s leading enterprises and governments to ensure AI can be deployed safely, responsibly, and at scale.

Our Values Define How We Protect What’s Next

Research First

Our origins are in discovery. Every advancement we make begins with deep technical research and a commitment to understanding the evolving AI threat landscape.

Integrity in Innovation

We build with transparency and responsibility because the security of AI impacts the security of everyone.

Collaboration with Purpose

Security is a team sport. We work alongside our clients, partners, and the broader cybersecurity community to strengthen the AI ecosystem together.

Relentless Curiosity

Every vulnerability is a puzzle waiting to be solved. We’re driven by curiosity and guided by impact.
