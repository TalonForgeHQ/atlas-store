Whole, human, behavioral health care

Improving the health of individuals and communities by enabling and delivering the best care for people in need.

See How

Delivering quality, whole-person care to those with behavioral healthcare needs - wherever, whenever, and however it's needed.

Quartet Health is an industry-leading value-based behavioral healthcare solution, now owned by NeuroFlow.  We expand access to and deliver high-quality care for people with behavioral health needs, including serious mental illness.  Quartet’s care enablement and delivery offerings seek to improve the lives of patients by providing whole, human, behavioral health care to those in need.

 

Our innovative approaches to navigating the complex mental health landscape enable us to identify, engage, and care for patients and deliver an end-to-end behavioral health solution.  We are a trusted partner of health plans, health systems, and community mental health centers that serve patients in 39 states across the country.
