Our story

We’re obsessed with making software quality easier.

Software innovation requires rapid iteration and learning, but testing is often the biggest bottleneck to faster releases. We created Testim because automated end-to-end testing is still too hard.

Testim is the leading AI-powered test automation platform for custom web applications. Testim enables fast authoring of AI-stabilized UI and end-to-end tests. Testim is also uniquely designed to help Agile teams efficiently manage and scale their automation projects. 

Testing solutions should fit into software teams’ tools and processes. That’s why we built Testim to be open and flexible. Everything is fully customizable, teams can connect to their CI or collaboration tools, and tests run on our grid or 3rd party testing grids.

Our solution is proven by hundreds of customers, including Microsoft, NetApp, and Sprinklr, running millions of tests on Testim each month.

In February 2022, Tricentis, the leader in continuous testing, acquired Testim. To read more about the acquisition and why there’s never been a better time to be a Testim customer, read this blog.
