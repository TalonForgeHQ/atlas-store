Our story

It started with the worst quarter ever

Years ago, Amit was the CEO of another company that just had its worst quarter ever. And they had no idea why. Amit soon realized that business decisions were based on guesswork; revenue leaders and sellers were only hearing part of the conversation. So he contacted his friend Eilon Reshef to see how they could use AI to understand what customers were actually telling them.

Growing together as Gongsters

We show up for each other at Gong. We embody our core values every day, in every interaction we have with our colleagues and customers.

Life at Gong

When Gongsters get together, it’s pure magic. Whether it’s regular team offsites, volunteering efforts, or an EOQ office party, we lift each other up and have fun doing it. We know there’s always work to be done, but still make time to celebrate our wins — no matter big or small.

Join the team

How we work

Our culture is built upon eight operating principles that guide how we work together day to day. These are our non-negotiables — our employees strive to live by them. Create raving fans, win as a team, and enjoy the ride are a few of our favorite ones.

Our operating principles

How we lead

While our operating principles guide us all, our leaders also follow core leadership principles that help us better support one another and foster individual and team growth. We strive to help everyone realize their full potential — these are the values that get us there.
