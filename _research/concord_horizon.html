AI-powered insights and analysis

Get instant AI answers about any contract

Get instant insights from your entire contract portfolio by asking natural questions—no more manual searching or filtering through hundreds of agreements to find patterns, risks, or opportunities.

AI-powered risk detection across all contracts

Instantly surface hidden risks like unfavorable auto-renewal clauses, missing termination rights, non-standard liability terms, and unusual payment conditions across your entire portfolio. Get proactive alerts about renewal deadlines, unfavorable clauses, and contracts that could be renegotiated for better terms.

AI-powered contract comparison at scale

Instantly spot differences, similarities, and key variations across multiple contracts by simply asking Concord to compare them—no more tedious side-by-side manual reviews.

A more customizable interface

Dark mode

Choose between Dark and Light mode for your interface. Available for your inbox, reports, deadlines and other data pages in (Q4). Settings and Document view available in 2026.

Full screen mode

Choose to pin or unpin the navigation, so you can visualize lengthy tables and data in full screen.

Your tables, your way

Pin columns, group them, reorder them… We added dozens of new features to let you work the way you want.

New filtering options

New fields to choose from

We've added new filterable fields including document status (signing, signed, approval, active, expired, renewed) and all lifecycle fields (effective date, expiration, renewal, termination notice). Filter using all key information about your documents.

New type of filters

Contains, doesn't contain, before, after, between, starts with, ends with… We added dozens of ways to filter your data in your inbox, reports and deadlines.

New ways to access filters

Filter a column directly by clicking it, or access a new side-panel that groups all filters available for your table.
