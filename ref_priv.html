    Thank you for your interest in Refersion! This Privacy Notice explains how Refersion collects, uses, discloses, and otherwise processes personal information in connection with our website, including refersion.com, and the related content, platform, services, products, and other functionality offered on or through our Services.

    When we use the terms “Refersion”, “we”, “us”, or “our” in this Privacy Notice, we are referring to Refersion, Inc., a New Jersey corporation. When we use the term “Service” or “Services”, we are referring to all of the services that we offer, including our product offerings and related platforms, affiliate marketplaces and related services, and the websites and mobile applications, if any, that post or link to this Privacy Notice.  Note that our Services are also subject to our Terms of Service.

    In some instances, we may conduct data processing activities on information relating to the Brands’ end users (“Brand Data”). In such instances, we have contractually committed ourselves to only process Brand Data on behalf of and under the instruction of the respective Brand, who is the “data controller”. This Privacy Notice does not apply to such processing and we recommend you read the privacy notice of the respective Brand if their processing concerns your personal information.

    From the first moment you interact with us, we are collecting personal information about you. Sometimes we collect personal information automatically when you interact with our Services and sometimes we collect the personal information directly from you. At times, we may collect personal information about you from other sources and third parties, even before our first direct interaction.

    We use commercially reasonable methods of protecting your personal information. Nevertheless, we cannot guarantee the safety or security of any personal information. Further, transmission of personal information cannot be made completely secure. For that reason, you agree to transmit personal information to us solely at your own risk. If you believe your personal information is being improperly used by us or any third party, please immediately notify us via email at [email protected].

    The personal information that we collect from you is dependent on the way in which you interact with our Services.

    Information for Visitors of Refersion.com

    When you interact with refersion.com or any related service sites, including marketplace.refersion.com, refersion.com or the like (all referred to herein as “refersion.com”), we may collect the following personal information you provide in connection with our Service:

    –   Contact Information: We may collect your contact information, including your full name, email address, mailing address and telephone number.

    –   Account Identifiers: When you register for an account, we collect certain information including your email address and password.  We may assign internal identifiers to your account to allow our systems to connect account information stored in different databases and tables. We may also collect your Contact Information and month and day of birth.

    –   Customer Service and Communication History:  When you contact us through any method of communications, including through our Service (such as the “Submit a request” link), via email, or through our chat bot, we may collect certain information including your name, address, email, Account Identifiers, Account History and the contents of your requests and your correspondence with us.

    –   Newsletters and Email Communication:  Our Service provides you an opportunity to sign up for our email communications by providing your email address.  We use your email address to communicate with you about our services and developments at Refersion, which may include marketing communications.  Please see the Your Communication Preferences section below for additional information about opting out of our marketing communications.

    –   Feedback Information:  We may also collect feedback and ratings you provide relating to our services, products and rewards, including reviews of products which may appear on our website.

    Although we often collect the personal information described above directly from you, we also collect this information through service providers and other third parties that collect it on our behalf, such as communications providers, analytics providers, and marketing providers.  In addition to the foregoing, we may make use of information provided by you or information regarding your interactions with refersion.com and its Brands in an anonymized format.  Such anonymized information may be used by Refersion for any purpose. Please see the Personal Information from Other Sources and Third Parties section below.
