## Five-question scope

### 1. Build-to-runtime lineage
Can a customer export one record joining the agent build, framework, model/version, deployment configuration, runtime identity, region, release time, and rollback target?

### 2. Tool-call authorization receipt
Can one connected-system action be joined to agent identity, tool, requested scope, policy version, authorization decision, credential boundary, downstream result, and denial or retry reason?

### 3. Unexpected-behavior trace and boundary enforcement
When an agent behaves unexpectedly, can the customer trace the decision path, identify the boundary that was evaluated, prove whether it blocked or allowed the action, and link the operator review or corrective action?

### 4. Observe/evaluate/experiment-to-release receipt
Can the customer join an observation or evaluation result to experiment configuration, reviewer decision, promoted release, scale event, and final production state without reconstructing the sequence from separate consoles?

### 5. Export and deletion proof
Can the customer export the evidence record and prove deletion across agent configuration, traces, evaluation artifacts, connected-system metadata, operational logs, caches, backups, and region replicas?
