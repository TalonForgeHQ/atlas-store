Hi there —

Showpad is the B2B SaaS revenue-effectiveness and sales-enablement platform built around the idea that field-sales teams — sellers who are physically in front of buyers — deserve the same content-distribution + conversation-quality surface that inside-sales teams have had for a decade. Merged in 2023 with Bigtincan (Australia), Showpad is now headquartered in Ghent, Belgium with US offices in Chicago and San Francisco, founded in 2011 by Pieterjan (PJ) Bouten and Louis Jonckheere, with Apratim Purakayastha as CEO, Kevin Collins as COO, Amanda Bedborough as CRO, Bernd Leger as CMO, and Leo Hart as CTO (verbatim first-party /about-us leadership roster 2026-07-22).
