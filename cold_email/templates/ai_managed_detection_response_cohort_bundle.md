1. **Detection-to-decision lineage:** Can an auditor trace one detection from source telemetry and customer tenant through detector or model version, AI enrichment, managed-SOC analyst identity, disposition, response action, rollback state, and final notification?
2. **Cross-product and provider handoffs:** Does every transition preserve stable joins across alert ID, case ID, connector and product version, tenant and region, analyst action, containment action, remediation ticket, and downstream customer acknowledgement?
3. **AI-to-human boundary:** Can the buyer distinguish AI-generated enrichment, policy-bounded automation, human validation, human override, failed or retried tool calls, and final analyst-approved disposition in one export?

5. **Regulatory export:** Can the record support SEC cyber 8-K Item 1.05 reconstruction, NIS2 Art. 21 and Art. 23 reporting, EU AI Act Art. 14 human-oversight review where AI is used, SOC 2 testing, ISO/IEC 27001, and ISO/IEC 42001 evidence requests without rebuilding the story from screenshots?
