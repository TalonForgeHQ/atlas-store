## Why Skyvern specifically

Browser agents concentrate untrusted web content, authenticated state, third-party model decisions, anti-bot boundaries and real external actions in one execution path. Skyvern adds a particularly auditable but easy-to-fragment decision layer: the vision LLM selects targets dynamically while Playwright performs the action, and customers can run either Skyvern Cloud or a self-hosted deployment. Procurement needs a joined record proving what the model saw, why it chose a target, which authenticated session acted, and what changed outside Skyvern.
