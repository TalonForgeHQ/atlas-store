Hi there —

Mindtickle is the revenue-enablement platform that turns rep readiness, content engagement, and sales coaching into measurable, role-aware behavior change. With ISO 27001 + ISO 27018 + ISO 27701 + SOC 2 + GDPR + HIPAA already in place, Mindtickle's procurement posture is solid; what we usually help teams close is the *audit replay* lane that connects each AI role-play session, each coaching conversation, and each readiness score back to the model, prompt, rep, and human manager review that produced it.

We built a compact procurement review that maps five evidence gaps teams commonly need before scaling a readiness + AI role-play fleet that touches Salesforce, Microsoft Dynamics, HubSpot, Gong, Chorus, Slack, and a long tail of internal LMS / content systems:

1. per-rep consent and retention evidence per AI role-play session, coaching conversation, and readiness assessment (Salesforce, Microsoft Dynamics, HubSpot, Gong, Chorus, Slack, internal LMS / content);
2. per-AI-roleplay-session provenance — which scenario, which model, which prompt produced each AI role-play transcript, coaching recommendation, or readiness score, with the manager-review or rep-acknowledgment step that authorized it;
3. join-tables between AI role-play sessions and the underlying CRM, content, call-recording, or scorecard record they touched, so a reviewer can replay a single readiness drill end-to-end;
4. role-based access and tenant isolation for cached AI role-play transcripts, coaching recommendations, readiness scores, and CRM-write-back payloads; and
5. reproducible review receipts for AI role-play runs and the manager review that authorized each one.

Mindtickle ships the controls worth checking: ISO 27001, ISO 27018, ISO 27701, SOC 2, GDPR, HIPAA. The gap we usually surface is the *audit replay* lane — proving which scenario produced a given readiness score or coaching recommendation and what manager review authorized it, across a full quarter of mixed AI role-play + coaching + CRM activity.

Would a one-page version of that review be useful for the person who owns enablement governance or revenue operations at Mindtickle?
