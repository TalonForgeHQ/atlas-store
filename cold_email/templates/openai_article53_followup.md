## Five-question audit opener

1. Can an enterprise customer export one immutable receipt tying each deployed model ID and release date to the exact system-card, model-card, evaluation, safety-policy, and training-data-summary versions reviewed for that release?
2. Can the same export prove the customer's processing region, retention setting, no-training setting, workspace identity, and applicable sub-processor list at the time of each material inference or fine-tuning job?
3. When a model, system card, policy, or evaluation changes, does the evidence pack preserve the prior version and show which customer deployments were affected instead of replacing the old state?
4. Can a downstream deployer reuse the pack for EU AI Act Article 53 and Annex IV diligence without reconstructing the chain from separate trust-center pages, contracts, screenshots, and support tickets?
5. Can procurement retrieve that evidence on demand as a machine-readable bundle with source URLs, timestamps, checksums, owners, exceptions, and remediation status?
