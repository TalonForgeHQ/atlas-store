## Five-question evidence replay

1. **Identity-to-owner lineage:** Can an auditor trace one human, workload, non-human, or AI-agent identity from source and sponsor through lifecycle state, credential or token, requested resource, policy version, entitlement, and final revocation?
2. **Decision and approval chain:** Does every access request preserve stable joins across request ID, policy and model version, recommendation, SoD result, approver, justification, ticket, conditional-access result, and final grant or denial?
3. **Privileged and agent action boundary:** Can the buyer distinguish eligible vs active privilege, just-in-time activation, AI-generated recommendation, autonomous agent action, human approval, human override, emergency stop, and rollback in one export?
4. **Failure-path retention:** Are denied, expired, retried, cancelled, overridden, orphaned, dormant, and rolled-back grants retained with the same fidelity as successful decisions, including actor, reason, timestamp, tenant, region, and final state?
5. **Regulatory export:** Can the record support SOC 2, ISO/IEC 27001, ISO/IEC 42001, NIST AI RMF, EU AI Act Art. 14 human oversight, NIS2 incident reconstruction, and SEC cyber disclosure evidence requests without rebuilding the story from screenshots?
