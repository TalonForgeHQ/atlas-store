5. **Regulatory export:** Can the record be exported for SOC 2 testing, NIS2 Art. 21 and Art. 23 reporting, EU AI Act Art. 14 human oversight and Art. 50 transparency, and SEC cyber-incident reconstruction without rebuilding the story from screenshots?
