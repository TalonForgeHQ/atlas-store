## Failure modes to avoid

- ❌ "I help companies with AI compliance" — too generic, instantly archived.
- ❌ Sending to info@ or hello@ when a real person exists — gates + spam filters eat it.
- ❌ Mentioning more than 5 gaps in the email — looks like a sales deck, not a consult.
- ❌ No CTA — reader doesn't know what to do next, defaults to "ignore."
- ❌ Pricing in the subject line — gets filtered as "spam/promo" by Gmail.
- ✅ Always lead with a specific gap count + specific regs + specific deliverable.
