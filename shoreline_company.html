Meet Shoreline

We're helping engineers improve their own work experience while increasing cloud service reliability for their customers.
Join us
We spent a lot of time on-call, squashing tickets at AWS. Shoreline is the tool we wish we'd had to eliminate tickets and improve availability.
Our purpose

Make on-call suck less

Reduce incidents

On call-teams don't need more dashboards. They need automation that reduces the number of incidents that they have to fix.

Slash escalations

Provide L1 teams with the right data and ability to launch pre-approved repairs, and your engineers won't see so many escalations.

Accelerate remediation

Automation is super fast, and on novel issues requiring humans, eliminating friction from infrastructure complexity cuts time to resolution.
