History,
Vision & Values

Giskard defends the vision of AI that can be trusted by its makers and users.   

We are committed to ensuring companies can deploy AI Agents with trust, by advancing research in AI safety and providing the leading standards for LLM quality & security in critical applications.
‍
Founded in Europe, we are Data Scientists, AI Engineers and AI Researchers who have spent years building & securing mission-critical AI systems for enterprises.

2021

We founded Giskard to address the challenges we faced in ensuring the performance, reliability and security of AI systems. Co-founders combine 25 years of experience building AI systems at Dataiku and Thales.

2022

We raised our first round of 1.5 M€ led by Elaia with participation from Bessemer & notable angel investors including the CTO of Hugging Face.  To learn more, you can read our announcement.

2023

We received a strategic investment of 3 M€ from the European Commission as part of the EIC Accelerator Horizon 2030 program. You can read more here.

2024

We won a 3 M€ grant from Bpifrance to lead research in evaluation & mitigation for Large Language Models (LLMs), in a collaborative consortium with Artefact, INA, BnF and Mistral AI. You can learn more here.

2025

We launched the Phare LLM Benchmark in partnership with Google DeepMind to evaluate LLMs across key safety & security dimensions. Trusted by enterprises like AXA, BNP Paribas, and Michelin, our testing platform secures customer-facing AI agents deployed to millions of users worldwide.

We believe in good engineering, collaboration & responsible innovation.
