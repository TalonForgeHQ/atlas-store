About

The Synthflow Story

Synthflow was founded in Berlin in 2023 with a focus on one problem: how to turn AI agents into systems that work reliably in real business environments.

We began with voice, solving the latency, telephony, and infrastructure challenges required to run human-like conversations in production. Over time, the platform expanded to support chat-based agents, enabling enterprises to automate conversations across channels without rebuilding their tech stack.

Today, Synthflow powers millions of customer interactions across industries, supporting multilingual teams and complex operational workflows. Backed by leading investors and a growing global team, we continue to focus on making AI agents dependable, scalable, and built for real-world use.
